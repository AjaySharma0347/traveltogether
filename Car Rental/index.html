About Us

TravelToGether is the world’s leading community-based travel network enabling over 90 million members to share a ride across 22 markets. TravelToGether leverages technology to fill empty seats on the road, connecting members looking to carpool or to travel by bus, and making travel more affordable, sociable and convenient. TravelToGether’s environmentally and human-friendly mobility network saves 1.6M tons of CO2 and enables 120M human connections every year.
